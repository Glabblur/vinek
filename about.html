Who We Are

KickHouse is for basketballers, by basketballers — and sneakerheads are very welcome to shop as well. Our store offers on-court performance-focused shoes as well as lifestyle sneakers, and promises top-tier quality at affordable prices. We want our customers to feel that they're getting the best on-court performance shoes or lifestyle sneakers without compromising affordability, satisfaction, and trendiness.

The Problem

We started KickHouse because we found that retail shoes were extremely expensive, even though retailers need to charge those prices to keep their businesses running. Even though we could find shoes from Carousell as a cheaper alternative, there are doubts whether they are real or fake. Additionally, if we are looking to buy trendy, new releases or limited edition shoes, it would also take long and grueling hours to research and source those shoes in particular, and make a risky decision to buy them without physically viewing and verifying their authenticity.

Our Solution

Hence, we started KickHouse to sell authentic basketball shoes and sneakers with extremely low markup costs and transparent pricing. Additionally, we sell the trendiest shoes based on the shoe market, as well as guarantee the authenticity of limited edition shoes, which are available for in-person viewing. We currently operate the store fully digital to reduce any unnecessary expenses, so that we can keep the pricing as transparent as possible.
